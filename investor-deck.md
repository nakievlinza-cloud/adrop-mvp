# One-liner
ADROP connects iGaming brands and affiliate teams with vetted UGC and banner creators,
with fast matching, escrowed payouts, and post-publication verification.

---

# Problem
- iGaming teams need short-form creatives fast, at scale, and with predictable outcomes.
- Creator sourcing is fragmented (Telegram chats, spreadsheets, agencies).
- Verification is manual and late; fraud risk is high.
- Time-to-creative is too slow for media buying cycles.

---

# Solution
ADROP is a marketplace + workflow system:
- Curated creators for UGC + banner/clip content
- Standardized offer creation and pricing models
- Escrow + disputes to reduce fraud
- Simple post-publication verification and hold logic

---

# Product Snapshot
- Roles: Brand / Affiliate team and Creator
- Offer types: UGC video, Banner/Clip
- Pricing models: Fixed, CPA, Barter
- Built-in messaging, notifications, and portfolio
- Verification + dispute flow (brand-side)

---

# Why Now
- Short-form channels dominate acquisition
- iGaming creatives refresh every 3-7 days
- Creator supply is growing, quality control is the bottleneck
- Brands want performance accountability, not just production
